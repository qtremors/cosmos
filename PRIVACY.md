# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 2025-12-14

---

## Overview

This privacy policy explains how **Cosmos** handles your data. Cosmos is a fully client-side application with **no data collection**.

---

## Data Collection

### What We Collect

| Data Type | Collected | Purpose |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| **Personal Info** | ❌ No | N/A |
| **Usage Data** | ❌ No | N/A |
| **Device Info** | ❌ No | N/A |
| **Local Files** | ❌ No | N/A |

### What We Don't Collect

- ❌ No personal information
- ❌ No usage analytics
- ❌ No cookies or tracking
- ❌ No external API calls
- ❌ No data sent to any server

---

## Data Storage

### 100% Client-Side

Cosmos runs entirely in your browser:

- **No Backend:** There is no server component
- **No Database:** No data is stored persistently
- **No External Services:** No third-party integrations

All computation happens locally in your browser using WebGL and JavaScript.

---

## Data Sharing

We **do not share any data** because we **do not collect any data**.

## Third-Party Services

Cosmos does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking.

| Service | Purpose | Used |

| Error tracking | Bug reporting | ❌ No |
